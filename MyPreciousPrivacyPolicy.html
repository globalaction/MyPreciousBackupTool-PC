My Precious Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

Welcome to My Precious

Welcome to use "My Precious". This app is developed by TAD Games and is committed to protecting your privacy.

Permission Requests

This app requests access to your camera and photo album to allow you to take photos and select pictures from your device for content creation.

Data Usage

The collected photos and videos are only used for display and editing within the app. All data is stored on your device and will not be uploaded to any servers or shared with third parties.

Permission Management

You can manage the camera and photo album access permissions for this app at any time in your device "Settings". Restricting access will not affect other functions of the app.

Children's Privacy

This app is for users of all ages and pays special attention to protecting the privacy of children under 13 years old, in compliance with relevant children's privacy protection regulations.
